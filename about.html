About Me

Henry is inspired by the impact design and technology has on the natural world.

He completed his Bachelor of Design in Product Design at the University of Technology Sydney in 2020. During this time, he had the chance to study at Delft University of Technology in the Netherlands, where he was taught the fundamentals of mechatronics. Further to this, Henry completed a Visual Design certificate from General Assembly, which covered UI, UX and branding.
